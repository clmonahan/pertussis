Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Visualizations

There does not appear to be any relationship between latitude and cloud cover in the above scatterplot. However, there are many cities at all latitudes that have 0% cloud cover, among other percentage groupings (i.e 20%, 40%, etc.). This could potentially be caused by the methodology used to classify levels of cloudiness. Judging by the locations of the bands, its possible that some weather locations use percentages to describe cloud cover, while otherse use oktas which express amount of cover in 1/8ths (or 12.5% groupings), but this is merely speculation and would require additional research to verify.
